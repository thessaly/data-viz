## B2 – Open Science Policies and Governance

### B2.1 — Share a machine-readable PDF file of the policy

National STI Policy 2013 – 2024, MoHERST

### B2.2 — Please, if possible, briefly explain the reason and the difficulties or obstacles in this area and indicate if there are plans to do so

The lack of open science policy, but there plans to review the National STI policy before end of 2025 and incorporate open science. However, we are faced with  some funding challenges to review the National STI policy.

### B2.3 — Please, if possible, briefly explain the reason and the difficulties or obstacles in this area and indicate if there are plans to do so

There are plans to promote open science but there is no specific policy instruments to do so.

### B2.4 — Please, if possible, briefly explain the reason and the difficulties or obstacles in this area and indicate if there are plans to do so

There are plans, but due to lack of policy instrument it is difficult to Implementation open science

### B2.6 — Please, if possible, briefly explain the reason and the difficulties or obstacles in this area and indicate if there are plans to do so

There are plans, but due to Lack of an Open Science Policy there are no funding mechanisms

### B2.7 — Please, if possible, briefly explain the reason and the difficulties or obstacles in this area and indicate if there are plans to do so

There are no funding mechanisms but when the policy is developed the funding mechanism will be put in place.

### B2.8 — Please, if possible, briefly explain the reason and the difficulties or obstacles in this area and indicate if there are plans to do so

There plans, but due to the lack of policy it remains a challenge
