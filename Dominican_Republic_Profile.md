### B4.3: Open science competencies in higher education curricula
Since the country made the commitment to promote open science at the 41st UNESCO General Conference, held in November 2021 in Paris, France, the country has been promoting and creating the conditions within higher education through panels, conferences, and other events. Although specific actions have been taken in the area of open science, there is no Framework of Open Science Competencies.

### B5.3: Initiatives to recognise/reward open science practices
The country adopted the Open Science Declaration, approved at the 6th Meeting of Ministers and High-Level Authorities of Higher Education, in Valencia, Spain, in October 2024, with the aim of promoting open science. The country has been promoting and creating the conditions within higher education through panels, conferences, and other events. Although specific actions have been taken in the area of open science, there is no prize or reward for Open Science practices. In this regard, it is worth noting that we are working on this matter to follow UNESCO's recommendations.
